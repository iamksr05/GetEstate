Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about buying, selling, and renting properties with GetEstate.

How do I list my property on GetEstate?

To list your property, simply click on the "List Property" button in the navigation bar. You'll need to create an account, provide details about your property (photos, description, price), and submit it for review. Once approved, your listing will be live for thousands of potential buyers to see.

Are there any fees for buyers?

No, searching for properties and contacting agents on GetEstate is completely free for buyers. We believe in open access to real estate information.

How are the property prices determined?

Property prices are set by the sellers or their agents. However, GetEstate provides market estimates and comparable sales data to help both buyers and sellers understand the fair market value of a home.

Can I schedule a viewing online?

Yes! On every property page, you'll find a "Schedule Tour" or "Contact Agent" button. You can propose a time that works for you, and the listing agent will confirm the appointment.

What is the "Verified" badge?

The "Verified" badge indicates that a GetEstate representative or a partner agent has physically visited the property and confirmed that the listing details and photos are accurate.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach our support team 24/7 via the Contact Us page. We also offer live chat support during business hours.
